Professional Career

The complete journey of a basketball career spanning over 13 years across multiple continents

2002-2006

College Basketball - Temple University

Played for the Temple Owls under legendary coach John Chaney. Named to All-Atlantic 10 First Team in senior year averaging 16.9 points, 4.7 rebounds and 3.8 assists per game.

2006-2008

New York Knicks

Drafted 29th overall in 2006 NBA Draft. Recorded a triple-double (13 pts, 10 reb, 10 ast) against Denver Nuggets in rookie season. Primarily used as defensive specialist.

2008-2010

Los Angeles Clippers

Traded to Clippers in 2008. Played 202 NBA games total with averages of 3.9 points, 1.9 rebounds and 1.6 assists per game.

2010-2011

KK Partizan (Serbia)

Won Serbian Cup and reached EuroLeague Final Four. Established reputation as elite European defender.

2011-2015

Lokomotiv Kuban (Russia)

Won EuroCup in 2013 and named to All-EuroCup First Team. Became team leader and fan favorite during 4-year stint.

2015-2019

Various European Teams

Played for Darüşşafaka (Turkey), Zenit Saint Petersburg (Russia), and AS Monaco (France). Known for veteran leadership and defensive expertise.
