Coaching Career Journey

From former professional player to elite coach - transforming teams and developing champions

2015-2017

Assistant Coach - Youth Development Program

Started coaching journey with Philadelphia Youth Basketball League. Focused on fundamentals and character development for ages 12-16. Developed innovative defensive training methods.

2017-2019

Head Coach - Metro High School

First head coaching position. Led team from bottom of conference to regional playoffs in two seasons. Implemented defensive system that reduced opponent scoring by 22%.

Won state championship in 2020. Named Coach of the Year in 2021. Sent 8 players to Division I colleges. Known for developing underrecruited players into college prospects.

2021-2022

Assistant Coach - Community College

Joined Riverside Community College as defensive coordinator. Team led conference in defensive efficiency. Specialized in player development and recruiting.

2022-Present

Head Coach - Elite Basketball Academy

Current position leading premier basketball development program. Focus on preparing players for college and professional levels. Maintains 95% college placement rate for graduating players.

Coaching Philosophy

Core beliefs and methodologies that drive championship results

Defense wins championships, but character builds champions. Every drill, every play, every decision is an opportunity to develop not just better basketball players, but better human beings.
- Coach Collins
Foundation

Defense First Mentality

Every great team starts with defense. We build from the ground up, emphasizing communication, positioning, and team concepts. Individual skills mean nothing without team chemistry.

Development

Player-Centered Growth

Each player is unique with different strengths and areas for improvement. We create individual development plans while maintaining team unity. Focus on maximizing potential rather than comparing players.

Character

Leadership and Accountability

Basketball is a vehicle for life lessons. We emphasize accountability, work ethic, and leadership. Players learn to support teammates, handle adversity, and celebrate success with humility.

Innovation

Adaptive Strategies

The game evolves, and so must we. We study film, analyze trends, and adapt our systems. Traditional fundamentals meet modern analytics to give our players every advantage.

Player Development Program

Comprehensive approach to developing complete basketball players
